Maasai Mara National Reserve



Maasai Mara (Masai Mara) is a beautiful national reserve in south-west Kenya

It is one of Africa’s Greatest Wildlife Reserves. A visitor will sourly be impressed by the diverse range of different animals.

It is one of the most spectacular eko-system in the world. Maasai Mara is one of the first destinations that you should take when you visit the beautiful country of Kenya.

Cultural practices of Kenya

Kenya is not a culturally and ethnically homogeneous country. Instead it is comprised of many different cultures.

Kenya consists of primarily 13 different ethnical groups and another 27 that are smaller. These cultures are all unique and different.

These cultures are all fascinating, they have their own cultures traditions and history.

Additionally around 13% of Kenyan population is non-African descent like Arabs, Indians and Europeans.

Kenyatta International Conference Centre

While most pieces of modern architecture are mostly comprised of steel glass and grey/white colors. Kenyatta International Conference Centre is unique in that regard with its beautiful but not generic looking design.

If you are an businessman then you surely should visit the Conference Center as it is one of the most renowned Conference centers of the world.

Additionally Kenyatta International Conference Centre is the tallest building in Kenya and it is the place where Kenyan parliament resides.
