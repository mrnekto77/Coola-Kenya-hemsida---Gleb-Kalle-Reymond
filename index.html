About

Kenya is a beautiful country located on equator in east Africa. Kenya is filled with many safaris and other natural habitats suited for African environment.


If you want to visit Africa then Kenya is the best place to visit as its wildlife is plentiful and contains creatures that you would usually expect from an African country.


Kenya was previously a colony of United Kingdom, but has since then broken free and is now a republic under the leadership of a president.


Kenya has also many attractions like for example Mount Kenya, which is Africa’s next biggest mountain after only Mount Kilimanjaro.
